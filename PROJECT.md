## Portfolio Story
A price monitoring tool that scrapes e-commerce sites, tracks product prices over time, and sends alerts on price drops. Shows scraping, data persistence, scheduling, and notification skills.

## Key Features to Showcase
- Multi-site scraping with anti-bot handling
- Price history tracking with SQLite storage
- Scheduled scraping (configurable intervals)
- Price drop alerts (email/webhook)
- CSV/JSON export
- CLI interface for managing tracked products
- Retry logic and error handling

## Standalone
Yes — pure Python, no external services
